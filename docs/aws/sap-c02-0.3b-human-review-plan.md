# SAP-C02 0.3B Human Review Plan

Status: checklist template; all content decisions pending
Design date: 2026-07-18

## Review objective

Human review must establish authority, factual accuracy, architectural quality, originality, distractor quality, answer uniqueness, compilation fidelity, rights, and release suitability for the exact two-lesson/five-question `SAP-ORG-04` pilot. This template records no content approval and names no reviewer.

## Reviewer roles

| Role | Minimum qualification/independence | Reviews |
|---|---|---|
| Source and rights reviewer | Can evaluate publisher identity, provenance, current project rights policy, intended use, and source freshness; no claim of legal counsel required | Source authority, rights/reuse, retained-snapshot policy, prohibited-source exclusion |
| AWS factual reviewer | Current demonstrable knowledge of Organizations, Control Tower, IAM Identity Center, CloudTrail, Config, Security Hub, RAM, and multi-account governance | Atomic documented facts, conditions, Region/account sensitivity, freshness |
| Architectural recommendation reviewer | Current multi-account architecture experience and ability to assess tradeoffs across isolation, autonomy, guardrails, workforce access, audit, delegation, operations, and cost | Derived recommendations, applicability, scenario realism, lesson architecture guidance |
| Question originality reviewer | Familiar with project rights boundary and independent item construction; must not have supplied recalled/live questions | Independent expression, no recognizable official/commercial scenario or close paraphrase |
| Distractor-quality reviewer | Understands professional item construction and relevant AWS behavior | Plausibility, parallelism, fairness, failure rationale, absence of trivia-only traps/cues |
| Answer-uniqueness reviewer | Qualified in both AWS multi-account architecture and requirement-option analysis; not the question author | Explicit requirements, key coverage, distractor failure, prioritizer, no hidden assumptions |
| Pack-release reviewer | Can verify exact versions/digests, scope, notices, approvals, compilation report, and non-claims | Final release projection and activation authorization |

One human may occupy several roles when each role/qualification/conflict statement is recorded separately. The author/model identity must remain distinguishable. A human who materially authors or rewrites a question cannot approve its uniqueness.

## Review packages

Reviewers receive only the material necessary for their role:

- source register entries, official URLs/locators, rights policy, and retained-snapshot metadata;
- atomic claims, derivation graphs, applicability/freshness fields, and validator reports;
- design specifications, final question drafts, requirement-option matrices, claim links, and rationales;
- originality provenance based on the abstract blueprint and independent drafting history, not unsafe source corpora;
- compiled candidate, release-evidence manifest, notices/rights, and validation output.

Private reviewer notes remain authoring-side. No AWS credentials, account data, private Skill Builder material, recalled questions, or unauthorized question bank is part of a review package.

## Review outcomes

Allowed decisions are `approved`, `changes_requested`, or `rejected`. An unresolved issue cannot be recorded as approval. Revisions create a new digest and invalidate affected downstream decisions according to [the approval model](sap-c02-0.3b-approval-model.md). All rows in this template remain pending until a later authorized content/review task supplies exact artifacts and human decisions.
