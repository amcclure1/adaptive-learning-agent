# Lesson Independent-Verification Protocol

Status: future required protocol

For every lesson revision, independently check that each material assertion maps to an exact approved and currently verified claim; no unsupported implication or new service behavior appears; applicability and caveats survive paraphrase; learner wording is accurate, original, non-misleading, and within objective scope; learner citations are faithful; and rights/freshness remain current.

The verifier records exact target/finding digests and cannot grant lesson-content review. Any prose or claim-mapping change invalidates the disposition and requires complete reverification before human lesson review.
