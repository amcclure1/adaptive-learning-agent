# Amateur Extra 0.2A Source Discrepancy

Date: 2026-07-18
Status: Content import paused pending explicit treatment in independent review

## Finding

The NCVEC consolidated **2024-2028 Extra Class FCC Element 4 Question Pool and Syllabus Public Release with 4th Errata Feb 4 2026** prints question E1A06 with official notation `[97.303(h)(1)]`.

Current 47 CFR § 97.303, as reverified on 2026-07-18, places the rule that a CW carrier on the discrete 60-meter channels is set to the center frequency in paragraph **(h)(3)**. Current paragraph (h)(1) instead identifies interference-protection obligations to United States and other nations' fixed services.

## Impact

- The E1A06 official ID, wording, option order, and answer key do not conflict with current Part 97.
- The keyed answer remains directly supported by current § 97.303(h)(3).
- The official NCVEC rule-reference notation appears stale after the 2026 Part 97 amendment.
- The pack must preserve the NCVEC notation as part of its source-question locator while citing current § 97.303(h)(3) for the project-authored explanation.
- Project prose must not silently present the current locator as though NCVEC printed it.

## Required independent-review treatment

Before the real pack approval record may be set to `approved`, an independent human reviewer must confirm this treatment against both authoritative sources and include it in the review notes. If NCVEC publishes newer errata before approval, the pack source baseline and golden transcription must be refreshed instead.
